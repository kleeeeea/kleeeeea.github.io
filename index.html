About me

I'm Keqian Li, and I am a Researcher at Meta (Facebook) AI. I obtained my PhD at the School of Engineering, University of California Santa Barbara, working with Prof. Xifeng Yan, Prof. William Wang and Prof. Ambuj Singh . Before that, I was an undergraduate student from the Special Pilot CS Class supervised by Andrew Yao at Tsinghua University.

I'm broadly interested in foundations and applications of representation learning. Specifically, I have been working on composing large capacity AI models to push the boundary of approximation, generalization and generalization following the pretraining fine-tuning scheme.
