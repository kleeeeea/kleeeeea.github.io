About me

I'm Keqian Li, and I am a Researcher at Meta AI. I obtained my PhD at the School of Engineering, University of California Santa Barbara, working with Prof. Xifeng Yan, Prof. William Wang and Prof. Ambuj Singh, and was affiliated with Google, Facebook, MSR, Yahoo! Labs and AOL in the past. Before that, I was an undergraduate student from the Special Pilot CS Class supervised by Andrew Yao at Tsinghua University.

I'm broadly interested in foundations and applications of representation learning. Specifically, I have been working on the soft-hardware codesign for composing large capacity AI models to push the boundary of approximation, generalization and generalization, with a particular focus on the pretrain-finetuning scheme.
